YOGA CLASS

Yoga class is typically a group fitness session led by a certified yoga instructor that focuses on teaching yoga poses, breathing techniques, and meditation practices. The class may begin with a brief period of centering and relaxation, followed by a warm-up sequence to prepare the body for more challenging poses.

The main portion of the class usually includes a series of standing, seated, and supine yoga postures, which may be held for several breaths or flow together in a continuous movement sequence. Throughout the class, the instructor will encourage students to focus on their breath and move mindfully, with awareness of their body and breath. The class may also include variations and modifications of poses to accommodate different levels of ability and experience. Towards the end of the class, students may be guided through a period of relaxation, such as Savasana, where they lie on their backs in a comfortable position and allow their bodies to fully release tension. The class may conclude with a short period of meditation or chanting, leaving students feeling relaxed, energized, and more centered.
